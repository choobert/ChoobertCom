FPS Continued
The last few days has led to the introduction of the crosshair, damage, and a few simplistic effects. When a player fires a shot, a small particle effect is played at the location of the shot, and a fading LineRenderer is drawn towards its destination. This allows for a pretty neat effect that looks as if sparks are flying out of the gun and a "bullet" is drawn for other players to know a shot has taken place. Also, based on where the shot has been taken (since it is a 3D sound), a sound clip is played. Interestingly enough, headphones will play them out of the correct side, right out of the box! Awesome!

Unfortunately current shots are taken out of the players head. This will be corrected in future posts.
